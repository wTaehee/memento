Dokkaebi (Korean Goblin)

Dokkaebi are like goblins, or naturally occurring deities from Korean lore who possess extraordinary abilities like shapeshifting in order to interact with humans. Dokkaebi maintains a contentious and playful relationship with their human counterparts, at times playing tricks on them, and others, helping them when facing mortal danger or financial hardship. The original form of dokkaebi, a gregarious masculine spirit, is often associated with objects of nostalgia, especially antiquated household objects and appliances that have made contact with blood, possibly, as recent studies from The Academy of Korean Studies suggest, menstrual blood.
